Privacy Policy

Understand how UtilHub collects and uses your data.

At UtilCentral, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our websites.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and messages submitted via contact forms. Additionally, we collect non-personal information including browser type, pages visited, and usage statistics to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries, requests, or support questions.
  • To provide and improve our tools and services.
  • To communicate important updates and announcements.
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior for optimization purposes.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our sites use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, remember preferences, and gather analytics data. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information with trusted partners only to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.
  • You can opt out of email communications at any time using the unsubscribe link.
  • For any concerns regarding your data, please contact us through our Contact Page.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
