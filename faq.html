**Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

Find quick answers to the most common questions about using UtilHub.

General Questions

Is UtilHub completely free to use?

Yes, absolutely! All core utilities on UtilHub are 100% free with no hidden fees or usage limits on the main tools. We are supported by non-intrusive advertisements.

Is my data secure when using the tools?

We prioritize your privacy. Most of our tools (like the Password Generator or JSON Formatter) process data entirely on your local machine (client-side) and do not transmit it to our servers. For cloud-based tools, we use secure, encrypted connections (SSL/TLS).

How often are new tools added to the platform?

We aim to add or update tools on a monthly basis. We actively monitor user requests and trends to prioritize the development of the most needed utilities. Check our Blog for the latest updates!

I found a bug or have a suggestion. Who should I contact?

We love feedback! Please use the 'Request/Suggest' link in the header or visit our Contact Us page to submit bug reports, improvement suggestions, or partnership inquiries.
