Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about using UtilHub and our tools.

General Questions

Is UtilHub completely free to use?

Yes! All core utilities are free with no hidden fees. Non-intrusive ads support the platform but do not affect your usage experience.

Is my data secure while using UtilHub?

Most tools operate directly in your browser, so your data never leaves your device. Cloud-based tools use encrypted SSL/TLS connections for privacy and security.

Do I need an account to use the tools?

No account is required. All tools are accessible without registration, keeping usage fast and frictionless.

Can I suggest a new tool or feature?

Yes! Click the 'Request/Suggest' link in the header to submit your ideas. We review suggestions regularly to prioritize development.

How often are new tools added?

We add or update tools roughly once a month. Follow our Blog for announcements of new utilities and updates.

What if I encounter a bug or technical issue?

Report bugs via the 'Request/Suggest' link or our Contact page. Include screenshots or steps to reproduce the issue for faster resolution.

Can I use UtilHub on mobile devices?

Yes! All utilities are mobile-friendly and optimized for smartphones and tablets.

Are the tools suitable for professional use?

Absolutely. UtilHub tools are designed for both personal and professional use, helping with productivity, document management, and online tasks.

Do the tools store my files or data?

Most tools process data in your browser, so nothing is stored on our servers. For tools that require upload, data is securely processed and deleted immediately after use.

Can I recommend UtilHub to my team or company?

Yes! Share our website link or consider our Partner/Advertise program to introduce UtilHub tools to your organization.

Are there any limits on tool usage?

Generally, there are no strict limits. Some advanced features may have soft limits to ensure fair usage and server performance, but core functionality remains unrestricted.
