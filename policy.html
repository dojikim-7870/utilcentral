Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 29, 2025

At UtilHub, accessible from utilhub.com, the privacy of our visitors is one of our main priorities. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by UtilHub and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect minimal information necessary for service functionality and improvement:

  • **Log Data:** IP addresses, browser type, ISP, timestamps, referring/exit pages.
  • **Non-Personal Data:** Anonymized usage statistics related to tool usage (e.g., number of times the QR generator was used).
  • **Client-Side Processing:** Most tools operate locally on your browser. Data entered into tools (e.g., passwords, JSON data) is *not* sent to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website and tools.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website and tool offerings.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website.
  • Communicate with you (via support, if requested).

3. Third-Party Partners

UtilHub uses third-party services, such as Google Analytics and advertising partners. These third parties have their own privacy policies.

4. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other laws to access, modify, or delete your personal data. Please contact us to exercise these rights.
